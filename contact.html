Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We provide services throughout Tanzania and have extensive networks across East Africa and international destinations.

How do I get a quote?

What documents do I need for customs clearing?

Required documents typically include commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, and import/export permits. Our team will guide you through the specific requirements.

Yes, we provide real-time tracking for all shipments, giving you full visibility of your cargo's location and status.
