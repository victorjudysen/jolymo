About Jolymo Logistics

Your Trusted Partner in Clearing & Forwarding Excellence

Our Story

Founded in Tanzania, Jolymo Logistics Company Limited has grown to become one of the region's most trusted clearing and forwarding companies. With over a decade of experience navigating the complex world of international trade, we've built our reputation on reliability, expertise, and unwavering commitment to our clients' success.

From our humble beginnings as a small customs clearance agency, we've expanded our services to cover the entire logistics spectrum—freight forwarding, warehousing, consultancy, and distribution. Our journey has been driven by one simple philosophy: your cargo's success is our success.

Today, we serve clients across East and Central Africa, handling thousands of shipments annually with the same attention to detail we gave our very first client. Our team of certified customs agents and logistics professionals work tirelessly to ensure your goods move smoothly across borders, helping your business thrive in the global marketplace.

Our Mission

To provide seamless, efficient, and reliable clearing and forwarding solutions that empower businesses to expand their global reach. We are committed to simplifying international trade through expert guidance, innovative logistics strategies, and unparalleled customer service.

Our Vision

To be the leading logistics partner in East and Central Africa, recognized for excellence in customs clearing, freight forwarding, and supply chain management. We envision a future where every business, regardless of size, can access world-class logistics support to compete in the global economy.
