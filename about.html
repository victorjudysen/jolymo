About Jolymo Logistics

Building bridges in global trade and logistics

Who We Are

Jolymo Logistics Company Limited is a leading logistics and supply chain solutions provider based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania, with expertise in clearing and forwarding across East and Central Africa. We specialize in cross-border transportation to Zimbabwe, Zambia, D.R Congo, Rwanda, and Burundi.

Our team of licensed customs brokers, freight forwarders, and logistics professionals brings together extensive knowledge of TRA regulations, EAC customs procedures, and regional transportation networks. We pride ourselves on delivering compliant, efficient, and personalized solutions for businesses of all sizes.

With strategic partnerships across the region and memberships in TAFFA (Tanzania Freight Forwarders Association), we have the networks, resources, and expertise to handle your most complex logistics challenges.
